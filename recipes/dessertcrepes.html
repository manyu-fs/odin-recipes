Dessert Crepes

This crepe recipe is essential for a fancy breakfast or eye-catching dessert.
Sprinkle warm crepes with sugar and lemon, or serve with whipped cream,
ice cream, and fruit.

  • Prep Time: 10 mins
  • Cook Time: 20 mins
  • Total Time: 30 mins
  • Servings: 8
  • Yield: 8 crepes

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 1/3 cups milk
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions

  1. Whisk together eggs, milk, flour, melted butter, sugar and salt in a large bowl
    until smooth
  2. Heat a medium skillet or crepe pan over medium heat. Grease the pan with a
    small amount of butter or oil applied with a brush or paper towel
  3. Using a serving spoon or small ladle, spoon about 3 tablespoons crepe batter
    onto the hot pan, tilting the pan to evenly coat the bottom surface. Cook until
    golden brown, 1 to 2 minutes per side; serve warm.
