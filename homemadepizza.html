Homemade Pizza

If you're looking for a homemade pizza crust recipe that's great for beginners,
you're in luck. This top-rated recipe is super easy to throw together on a whim,
and it puts the store-bought stuff to shame. Learn how to make the best pizza
crust of your life with just a few ingredients, find out how to shape the dough,
and get our best storage secrets.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm water at 100째F or 45째C
  • 1 package active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 2 1/2 cups bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 450째F or 230째C. Lightly grease a pizza pan.
  2. Gather all ingredients
  3. Place warm water in a bowl; add yeast and sugar. Mix and let stand until
    creamy, about 10 minutes.
  4. Add flour, oil, and salt to the yeast mixture; beat until smooth. You can do this
    by hand or use a stand mixer fitted with a dough hook to make it easier.
    Let rest for 5 minutes.
  5. Turn dough out onto a lightly floured surface and pat or roll into a 12-inc circle
  6. Transfer to the prepared pizza pan
  7. Spread crust with sauce and topping of your choice.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, 15 to 20 minutes. Remove from the oven and let cool for 5 minutes before serving.
